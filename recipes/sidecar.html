Sidecar

Description

It’s now common practice to sip cognac neat. It sure does shine that
way. But you’ll gain a new appreciation for the remarkably versatile
French spirit after you try it in this citrusy cocktail, a mixture of
cognac, fresh lemon juice, and the orange liqueur Cointreau. Don’t balk
at the sugar-coated rim: It’s there to mask the Sidecar’s wicked tongue.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz cognac
  • 0.75 oz Cointreau
  • 0.75 oz lemon juice
  • Garnish: orange twist
  • Garnish: sugar rim

Steps

  1. Dip the rim of coupe glass in lemon juice, then coat with sugar
  2. Add the cognac, Cointreau, lemon juice, and ice to a shaker, then shake
  3. Strain into the prepared coupe glass
  4. Garnish with orange twist
